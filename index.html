I am delighted to present a collection of new works in the Copper House Gallery in Dublin on 28th September. The exhibition two years in the making, will present a wide range of subjects in various media. I am grateful to Arekibo Communications for their support in sponsoring this event.

All works are for sale with 30% of all proceeds being donated to Jigsaw - the National Centre for Youth Mental Health.
