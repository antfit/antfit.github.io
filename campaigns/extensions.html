About the Copper House Gallery

The Copper House Gallery is in St Kevin's Cottages, Synge Street.

Ireland's longest established photographic and fine art printing studio, working with photographers, artists, curators and galleries making the finest museum quality prints and exhibitions.

Jigsaw (formerly Headstrong) is the National Centre for Youth Mental Health in Ireland with the mission to bring about significant change in how Ireland thinks about, responds to and supports young people's mental health.

Event supported by Arekibo

Everyday Jigsaw deliver a range of vital services to improve the lives of young people living with mental health issues and those around them. In addition, they undertake ground-breaking, collaborative, high-impact youth mental health research, provide the most up-to-date information about youth mental health and fight for change and raise awareness of youth mental health on a national level.

Established in 2006 by Dr. Tony Bates to respond the growing crisis in the area of youth mental health, Jigsaw today represent an innovative approach to promoting and supporting youth mental health, in line with best practices of clinical, financial and operational governance.

We are delighted to partner with Jigsaw for this exhibition and to contribute, not just to Jigsaw as an organisation, but to directly impact how we as country, thinks about, supports and responds to our young people's mental health.
